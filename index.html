BidSpot

BidSpot is an internal GPU marketplace MVP demo. It simulates real-time auctions, job queueing, and market clearing for compute resources.

This frontend-only MVP is designed to showcase the product idea to investors and stakeholders. It demonstrates auction cadence, clearing prices, and team job submission. Connect it to your backend APIs for a production-ready experience.
